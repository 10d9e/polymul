## Approach

Replace naive O(N^2) schoolbook with an O(N log N) negacyclic NTT.
Three NTT-friendly primes (998244353, 1004535809, 985661441), each with 2048 | (p-1) and primitive root 3, carry a length-1024 negacyclic transform via psi pre/post weighting (psi a primitive 2N-th root). Forward transform is decimation-in-frequency and the inverse is decimation-in-time, so no bit-reversal permutation is needed. Per-prime residue products are recombined with Garner CRT; since |signed product coefficient| < 2^74 and P0*P1*P2 ~= 2^89.7, the exact integer is recovered and reduced mod 2^32 (sign decided by the top mixed-radix digit, no u128 in the hot path).
All twiddle/psi/CRT tables are precomputed in plan_new (free under the differential fuel metric). Hot loops keep residues < 2^32 so reductions are single u64 % ops, and use get_unchecked to drop bounds checks.
