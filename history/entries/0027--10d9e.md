## Approach

Make the spectral **pointwise product** division-free, removing the last `% p`
in the hot loop. The two operands are both variable, so Shoup (which needs a
fixed multiplier) doesn't apply — instead carry the whole spectral domain in
**Montgomery form** (R = 2^32). The psi pre-weight table is scaled by R and the
psi^{-1}*N^{-1} post-weight table by R^{-1}, so values enter Montgomery form at
the pre-weight and leave it at the post-weight; the twiddles and butterflies are
untouched because Montgomery-form * normal stays Montgomery-form. The pointwise
is then a Montgomery multiply (`pinv = -p^{-1} mod 2^32` precomputed via Newton),
~3 multiplies + a shift + one conditional subtract, no division. The hot path is
now entirely free of integer division (`%` survives only in the free plan_new).
