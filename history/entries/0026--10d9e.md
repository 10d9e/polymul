## Approach

Eliminate the per-input coefficient reduction. The forward transform previously
did `a[j] % p` before the negacyclic psi pre-weight. But `shoup_lazy` is valid
for any operand `< 2^32`, so the raw u32 coefficient can be fed straight in:
`shoup_lazy(a[j], psi^j, ...)` computes `a[j]*psi^j mod p` in `[0,2p)` directly,
folding the reduction into the multiply that was happening anyway. Removes one
`% p` (the most expensive op, weight 25) per input coefficient across all six
forward transforms — with identical outputs.
