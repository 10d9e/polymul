## Approach

Fuse the inverse final DIT stage + post-weight into the Garner CRT
Previously each prime ran its full inverse (including the final radix-4 stage
and psi^-1 post-weight), wrote its result array r_k to memory, and then the CRT
read all three back. Now each prime stops one stage early (convolve_prefinal),
and a single fused pass (final_crt) runs the final butterfly + post-weight for
all three primes in registers (final_prime) and CRT-combines the four resulting
coefficient groups on the spot (crt_one). The three per-prime result arrays are
never written or read back — eliminating ~6N of charged load/store traffic.
wasm locals are free under the metric, so the extra live state costs nothing.
