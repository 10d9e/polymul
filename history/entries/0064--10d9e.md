## Approach

Fuse the entire forward middle (len=256+64+16) into one radix-64 pass
Extends the radix-16 forward fusion to absorb the remaining separate len=256
stage (which also carried the psi pre-weight). The forward middle is now a
single radix-64 = radix-4(len=256) ∘ radix-4(len=64) ∘ radix-4(len=16) pass:
for each jj in 0..16 there is exactly one butterfly that gathers the 64
elements at positions jj + k*16 (the whole spectrum, stride 16) into 64 named
locals, applies the pre-weight in the gather, runs the three radix-4
sub-stages entirely in registers, and scatters back. This removes another full
store+load of the spectrum (the old stage1 -> radix16 xab round-trip).
The 64-element tile is held in 64 explicit named locals, which — as with the
radix-16 tiles — keeps it in (free) wasm locals under the strided gather rather
than spilling to linear memory; an array form fails SROA. Sub-stage twiddles:
len=256 group g uses w^{jj+16g}; len=64 group l uses w^{4jj+64l} (shared across
the four 256-blocks); len=16 shares w^{16jj}.
Bit-identical (same wasm checksums; native gate and all 32 fixtures pass).
