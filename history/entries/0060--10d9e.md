## Approach

Vectorize pack_ab: build 4 (a,b) lane-pairs per iter via load+shuffle+widen
The (a,b) operand packing built one i64x2 lane-pair per element with a
scalar L::new (two u32 loads + two zero-extends + a lane construct, ~13
weighted ops/element). Replace with L::pack4: two v128 loads of a[0..4]
and b[0..4], two i32x4 interleave shuffles, and four u64x2 zero-extending
widenings produce four lane-pairs at once (~2.5 ops/element). pack_ab is
built once and shared across all three primes, so this is pure overhead
removed. WORK 8771024 -> 8668624.
